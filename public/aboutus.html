About Us

Welcome to Mrucha Beauty — a brand born from passion, creativity, and the dream of bringing bold, feel-good beauty to everyone. Founded by content creator Mrunal, Mrucha Beauty is all about celebrating individuality through vibrant, nourishing lip colors that feel as amazing as they look.

Our products are thoughtfully crafted to combine rich, high-impact color with lip-loving ingredients, so you can enjoy a smooth, comfortable finish that lasts. Every shade is designed with care, blending quality and style to help you express yourself in new and exciting ways.

At Mrucha Beauty, we believe that makeup should do more than just look good. It should empower you, bring you joy, and let your unique personality shine through. Thank you for being a part of this journey—let’s make every day a little bolder, a little brighter, and a lot more beautiful.
